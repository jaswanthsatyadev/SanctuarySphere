Our Mission

At Sanctuary Sphere, we are committed to protecting and preserving wildlife while fostering a deep connection between people and nature. Our mission is to safeguard endangered species, restore natural habitats, and inspire conservation awareness for a sustainable future.

Our Story

Established in 1980, Sanctuary Sphere began as a humble effort to provide a safe haven for threatened species. Over the years, it has flourished into a 1million acre sanctuary, housing over 10,000+ species of animals and plants. Through dedication, partnerships, and support from wildlife enthusiasts, we’ve grown to become a symbol of hope and resilience for conservation efforts.

What Makes us Unique

-> Rare and Endangered Species: Our sanctuary is proud to protect species, that are on the brink of extinction.
-> Lush Natural Habitat: Spread across 1million, we offer a natural, thriving environment for the flora and fauna we protect.
-> Programs: From wildlife rehabilitation to eco-tourism and educational tours, we work tirelessly to connect visitors with nature and teach the importance of conservation.

Our Impact

At Sanctuary Sphere, we measure our success not just in numbers but in the lives we’ve transformed and the ecosystems we’ve restored.

-> Over 10,000 animals rescued from poaching, habitat destruction, or injury.
-> Provided long-term care for endangered species
-> Conducted workshops and community outreach, impacting local communities.
-> Reduced human-wildlife conflict in surrounding areas
